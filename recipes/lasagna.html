Lasagna

This is a delicious lasagna recipe!

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

It is a hearty and satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the meat sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
