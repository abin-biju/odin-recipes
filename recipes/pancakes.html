Pancakes

Fluffy homemade pancakes that are perfect for breakfast.

Pancakes are a quick and easy breakfast option made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder.

Serve them warm with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit for a delicious morning meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the griddle to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of your choice.
